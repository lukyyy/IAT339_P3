Adventure of Pergie

IAT 410 Final Project

Tool Used

Unity

Role

Director/Coder

Goal:

Creating/Designing interactive game using unique concept.


Overview

Adventure of Pergie is a 2D platformer puzzle game where the main character, Pergie tries to reunite with his mother. Pergie need to pass through obstacles, colored walls, to meet his mother once again.
The game provide both PC and Mac format.

A sample game play

Concept

Pergie's ability is that he is able to penetrate walls that shares same color with him. Also, Pergie can store up to three color for himself and change the color in order of he collected. There are several obstacles such as spikes, boxes, and walls that prevent Pergie to go higher to meet his mother.
But by using his ability, Pergie can go up higher and higher.

One of story scenes in game

Process

As a director/coder of this project, I was responsible for deciding entire concept of the game and how the each puzzle in the game works such as level design, leading players, and others.
Also, for coding responsibility, I have implemented most of the mechanics in the game though C++ with Unity. The core mechanic, being able to penetrate through wall was the trickest part overall and level designs.
